Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Who we are

Cobbler Café is a dessert café located at 34 Kote Afkhazi Street, Old Tbilisi, Georgia. This privacy policy explains how our website (cobblercafe.ge) handles data when you visit it.

We do not run advertising, we do not sell data, and we do not use analytics services. This site exists to show you our menu and tell you how to find us.

Data we collect directly

We collect no personal data directly. This site has no contact forms, no newsletter sign-up, no login, and no order system. Any orders placed through Wolt are handled entirely by Wolt under their own privacy policy.

Third-party services

This site loads content from third-party providers. When these load, your browser makes a request to their servers, which means your IP address is sent to them. Below is a full list:

  • Google Fonts — We load the Playfair Display typeface from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com). This sends your IP address to Google. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Maps — The map on our homepage is an embedded Google Maps iframe. It only loads if you accept cookies. When it loads, Google may set cookies and receives your IP address.
  • Wolt — The "Order on Wolt" buttons link to Wolt's external website. We share no data with Wolt; their privacy policy applies once you click through.
  • Instagram — The "@cobbler_cafe" button links to Instagram. No data is shared until you click.

Cookies

We set one cookie of our own:

  • cobbler_cookie_consent — Stores your accept or decline choice so we don't ask again. Stored in your browser's localStorage. Contains no personal data. Never sent to any server.

If you accept cookies, Google Maps may set its own cookies when the map loads. These are Google's cookies, governed by their policy.

Google Fonts does not set persistent cookies but does receive your IP address on each page load as part of the font request.

Your rights

Under GDPR (if you are in the European Economic Area) and similar laws, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is held about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

Since we hold no personal data directly, most requests will need to be directed to the third-party services listed above. For anything related to our site specifically, contact us at the address below.

Withdraw or change consent

You can reset your cookie choice at any time. This removes your saved preference and shows the cookie notice again on your next visit.
